Assemble the smart bandage

Please follow the following steps:

    1. Please insert the battery into the smart bandage.

    2. Place the sensors on the “sticky” side of the secondary dressing.

    3. Remove hydrogel (the "jelly-like substance) from packaging and place it over the sensors.

    4. Attach assembled smart bandage to your wound, with the hydrogel side in contact with your wound.
