Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Please click on the questions to view the answer

Question 1: How do I put on the smart bandage?
Place the bandage on the wound like any other traditional bandage. For the velcro, place it on any part of the leg as to your preference. Green LED will ensure that the circuit board in the casing is connected to the smart bandage.
Question 2: What is being assessed to inform me that my wound is infected or not?
Temperature and pressure are being assessed by the sensors embedded in the smart bandage. These physiological conditions can help determine if the wound is infected or not.
Question 3: How often should I change the smart bandage?
Average battery life span of the smart bandage is around 5 to 10 hours, after that please change to another new battery.
Question 4: Are there any potential side effects while using the smart bandage?

Question 5: Is there any special maintenance required for the smart bandage?
Even though there is a casing covering the circuit board, we would highly recommend not to have any part of the device in contact with water to prevent any injuries.
Question 6: The LED on the casing is NOT GREEN, what does that mean?
Green LED is an indication that the wound temperature is good and pressure is good. Blue LED is an indication that the wound temperature is high and pressure is good. Purple LED is an indication that the wound temperature is good and pressure is high. Red LED is an indication that the wound temperature is high and pressure is high. If the LED colour is not green, we strongly advise you to visit a doctor ASAP.
