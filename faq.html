Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question 1: How do I put on the smart bandage?

Place the bandage on the wound like any other traditional bandage. For the velcro, place it on any part of the leg as to your preference. Ensure that the circuit board in the casing is connected to the smart bandage.

Question 2: What is being assessed to inform me that my wound is infected or not?

Temperature and humidity is being assessed by the sensors embedded in the smart bandage. These physiological conditions can help determine if the wound is infected or not.

Question 3: How often should I change the smart bandage?

Average battery life span of the smart bandage is around 5 to 10 hours, after that please change to another new battery.

Question 5: Is there any special maintenance required for the smart bandage?

Even though there is a casing covering the circuit board, we would highly recommend not to have any part of the device in contact with water to prevent any injuries.

Question 6: The LED on the casing is red/yellow, what does that mean?

A red/yellow LED in an indication that the wound temperature or wound humidity respectively is above the optimum temperature, do seek medical help to change the bandage.
