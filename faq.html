Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question 1: How do I put on the smart bandage?

Place the bandage on the wound like any other traditional bandage. For the velcro, place it on any part of the leg as to your preference. Ensure that the circuit board in the casing is connected to the smart bandage.

Question 2: What is being assessed to inform me that my wound is infected or not?

Temperature and humidity is being assessed by the sensors embedded in the smart bandage.

Question 6: The LED on the casing is red/yellow, what does that mean?

A red/yellow LED in an indication that the wound temperature or wound humidity respectively is above the optimum temperature, do seek medical help to change the bandage.
